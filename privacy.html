Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

CozySwap respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the CozySwap decentralized exchange protocol (“the Protocol”).

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CozySwap is a fully decentralized, non-custodial protocol. We do not collect or store personal information such as names, emails, or wallet private keys. All interactions occur directly on the blockchain.

2. On-Chain Data

All transactions performed through CozySwap are public and recorded on the blockchain. This data is immutable and can be viewed by anyone using public blockchain explorers. CozySwap does not control or modify on-chain information.

3. Cookies and Analytics

CozySwap’s main interface does not use cookies or analytics trackers that collect personally identifiable information. However, third-party tools or web hosting providers may use limited cookies for functionality or security.

4. Third-Party Links

CozySwap may include links to third-party sites or applications. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their policies before interacting with those platforms.

5. Security

While CozySwap does not store personal data, we encourage users to take security precautions — such as safeguarding private keys and using reputable wallets. We cannot recover lost or stolen digital assets.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

For questions or feedback, please reach out through CozySwap’s official community channels.
